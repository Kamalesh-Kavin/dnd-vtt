Welcome to Dungeon Crawl VTT!

You're about to explore a procedurally generated dungeon, fight monsters, find treasure, and descend deeper into the darkness.

This guide will teach you everything you need to know. No tabletop RPG experience required!

This tutorial will only appear once. You can reopen it anytime with the ? button on the game screen.
🚶

Moving Around

Click a tile Click anywhere on the map to move there. Your character will automatically find the best path.
Pan the map Click and drag to move the camera. Use the scroll wheel (or +/- buttons) to zoom.
Path preview Hover over a tile to see the path your character will take (golden dots) and the number of steps.
Fog of War Dark areas haven't been explored yet. Move closer to reveal them! Your vision has a limited range.
🗺

Map Legend

Floor Safe to walk on
Wall Can't pass through
Door Walk through to open — may lead to new rooms
Stairs (>) Descend to the next dungeon level
Chest ($) Walk over to loot — gold, potions, or weapons!
Traps Hidden! Look like floor tiles. High Wisdom helps you spot them.
⚔

Combat

How it starts Combat begins automatically when you get close to a monster. Everyone rolls initiative to determine turn order.
Your turn When it's your turn, you'll see action buttons: Attack, Cast Spell, or End Turn. Choose a target from the dropdown.
Attacks A d20 is rolled + your modifier. If it meets or beats the target's AC (Armor Class), you hit! Then damage is rolled.
Monster turns Monsters act automatically. They'll attack the nearest player or move closer if they're out of range.
✨

Spells & Dice

Spells Spellcasters (Wizard, Cleric, Bard, etc.) can cast spells. Cantrips are free; higher-level spells use spell slots that recharge when you descend stairs.
Dice roller Use the dice buttons in the sidebar to roll any die. You can also type /roll 2d6+3 in chat!
Ability scores STR (melee), DEX (ranged/AC), CON (HP), INT (wizard spells), WIS (cleric spells/perception), CHA (bard spells)
Healing Find Potions of Healing in chests, or have a Cleric cast healing spells. Your party rests when descending stairs.
💡

Tips for Survival

Explore carefully Move through rooms systematically. Traps are hidden, so characters with high Wisdom are valuable scouts.
Watch your HP Keep an eye on your health bar. If you hit 0 HP, you start making death saves. Three failures and you're gone!
Descend when ready Find the stairs (golden > symbol) and click the "Descend" button. Your party will rest and heal slightly before the next floor.
Read the Adventure Log The sidebar shows all narration and combat results. The AI Dungeon Master describes what's happening — pay attention for clues!
Good luck, adventurer! May your rolls be high and your HP plentiful.
